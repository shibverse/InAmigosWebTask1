About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive social change. The organization works in areas such as education, women empowerment, environmental conservation, animal welfare, and community development through various impactful projects.

Our Mission

To empower communities through education, social welfare, environmental protection, and volunteer-driven initiatives.

Our Vision

To build a compassionate and empowered society where every individual has the opportunity to grow and contribute.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project SEVA

Providing food, clothes and humanitarian support to underprivileged communities during emergencies and daily needs.

Project Bachpanshala

Helping children receive quality education through free learning programs, digital literacy and educational support.

Project Jeev

Protecting and caring for stray animals through feeding drives, rescue initiatives and awareness campaigns.

Project Prakriti

Promoting environmental conservation through tree plantation campaigns and awareness programs.

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development, awareness and self-reliance initiatives.

Project Vikas

Providing internship opportunities and skill development programs for students and young professionals.

Our Social Impact

Through our dedicated projects and volunteer network, InAmigos Foundation strives to create meaningful and lasting change in communities across India.
